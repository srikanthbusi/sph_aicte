About AICTE

The All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) was established in November 1945 as a national-level apex advisory body to promote development in technical education across India. In 1987, it gained statutory status through an Act of Parliament, enabling it to play a more effective role in shaping technical education policies and standards.
